About

Shane's passion for Electronic Dance Music (EDM) led him to explore a wide range of genres. His DJ sets now feature an energetic mix of Rock/Retro, Hip-Hop, Commercial, Techno, Psytrance, and Bollywood, with a strong inclination toward high-BPM music. His ability to blend these styles keeps his performances fresh and engaging. With three years of experience, Shane has mastered the art of reading crowds and setting the perfect vibe for any venue, from clubs to private events. To enhance his technical skills and industry knowledge, he earned a DJ certification from Flying Chords in Andheri Lokhandwala, further solidifying his expertise in mixing, transitions, and live performance techniques.
